ABOUT PRASAD GYM

Prasad Gym Studio enrolled into Mission Fit India Programme and is awarded as the Best Gym in Siwan. We’re a Community, it’s a base for improving overall Personality & to be healthy with a motive of Disease Free Nation. We believe that a body only achieves what the mind believes, So We focus on shredding down your Fat & gaining Lean Muscles along with Improving your Mental Strength, Lifestyle & Confidence. We’re a Brand "Cross Fit Studio" - Best unisex Gym in Siwan, Bihar. Our Motto is to make everyone understand that gym is not just for Weight Loss & Weight Gain, It’s the necessity to be fit.

We run on the Result Oriented Process with different levels for beginners, intermediate & Advance levels with Proper Evaluation through Body composition Test on the consistent basis. Diet Chart is made on the basis of your personal goal & Present body Condition with Dietician’s Consultancy. The Training is designed through Certified Trainers in a healthy way according to your goals. Join the Fitness Revolution!. We are the best gym in Patna offering services like body transformation, functions training, muscle building, weight loss/gain, cross fit training, aerobics training, power yoga, pain relief workout and other fitness programs
